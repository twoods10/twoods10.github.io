How Connect the Dots works

Step One

Search for the standard, topic, or grade level that you are teaching.

Step Two

View the prerequisite content standards from the previous grade in order to identify areas where your students may need to build foundational understanding before moving into the grade-level content. OR, view the highest (teacher)-voted resources for teaching that standard.

Step Three

Follow the link to that resource and cast your own vote! It may be a video, lesson plan, task, or assignment that other teachers have used and found helpful in building understanding for their students.
